Players from abroad (info in english)

29.04.2015 20:43:29

Info for e.g. swedish players. 

Welcome to Murhaniemi Open I in Kemi. Välkommen till Mordkap Open I. Competition is held at Ajos Disc Golf Course. We play two times 18 holes. 

 

We have also upcoming accommodation offers so follow these pages! In saturday 13 th of June there is a couple competition at 5 pm finnish time and some clinics earlier that day. 

Registaration is open at the moment! You have to have be PDGA member of year 2015 and member of your countrys DGA. Register at Finnish DGA website: https://kisakone.frisbeegolfliitto.fi/ or top right of this page.

If you have questions about registration or anything else, feel free to mail: jatkan.kiekko@gmail.com. We answer fairly quickly. 

In order to register for our competition, you need to be logged in and ready at 6 pm. Refresh the page and quickly press "Ilmoittaudu", when it appears on the line of our info. Choose the right class and you should do fine. If you have problems or don't get in, you have always the option to apply for wild card in that same email address up there. 

We welcome you all to Kemi!
