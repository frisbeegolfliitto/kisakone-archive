In English

Mukkula Open Sponsored by Westside Discs is played for the third time in Lahti, Finland. For the past two years the competition course has housted the Finnish national disc golf championships in 2015 and the Finnish PRO-tour event in 2016.

Event information

  • Dates 10.-11.6.2016
  • PDGA A-tier
  • 144 players in the following player divisions (+ amount of players in each division)
    • MPO 48, FPO 12, MPM 24, FPM 6, MPG 10, MPS 4, MA2 28, FA1 6, MJ2 6
  • 3 rounds of 18 holes for everyone
    • 2 rounds on Saturday 10.6.2016 (group A and group B)
    • 1 round on Sunday 11.6.2016
  • Unofficial doubles play on friday 9.6.2016
    • Everyone can participate
    • 2 divisions, mens doubles and mixed doubles
    • one round of 18 holes, best shot
    • 10€ per pair, payout 100% (40/30/20/10) for top4 in each divisions

Contact information

  • Tournament director: Eero Leinonen, lfcsihteeri@gmail.com, +358505458736
  • Assistant tournament directors: Joona Laajoki and Jani Laamanen

Tournament payouts

The competition will have the following prizes

PRO divisions

  • Money payouts in all PRO divisions (can be changed into gift cards for AM players)
  • Payout 75% and top 25% players will be awarded
  • If all pro divisions will be full, then the pro purse will be around 5500€ (including added cash 1000€)
  • The added 1000€ cash will be devided top heavy in pro divisions
  • Top 3 from each division gets trophys

Amateur divisions

  • All amateurs will get a players package
    • At the moment it includes: Westside Elasto Harp, Westside towel, Westside drinking bottle, Westside sacbag
  • Top3 in all amateur divisions will receive product prizes and also top 3 will get trophys

Tournament registration

  • The tournament has a 4 phase registration
  • A foreing player needs to have a valid PDGA-membership
  • Register into https://kisakone.frisbeegolfliitto.fi/
    • Please use an active email account in the registration
  • Sign up for the competition in here https://kisakone.frisbeegolfliitto.fi/kilpailu/682/katso/ilmoittautuminen
    • You will find the correct divisions where to sign by scrolling down. Do notice the rating limitations during each phase of the registration
    • If you run into problems, contact the TD

Tournament fee:

  • 60€ MPO, FPO, MPM, FPM, MPG, MPS
  • 30€ MA2, FA1, MJ2

Registration schedule - IMPORTANT

Tournament is full at the moment. If you wish to join the waiting list, then please send an email to the TD lfcsihteeri@gmail.com (who you are, what division)

Stage 1: 1st of March at 18.00 to 6th of March

  • MPO 950+ rating, MPM 935+ rating, FPO 850+ rating (FPM, MPG, MPS, MA2, FA1, MJ2 all ratings)
  • Spots will be filled in registration order
  • Stage 1 registration payments must be received by the organizer by 10th of March
  • Payments will be checked during 11th and 12th of March and players who have not paid the fee will be removed from the competition

Stage 2: 13th of March at 18.00 to 19th of March

  • MPO 930+ rating, MPM 900+ rating (FPO, FPM, MPG, MPS, MA2, FA1, MJ2 all ratings)
  • Spots will be filled in registration order
  • Stage 2 registration payments must be received by the organizer by 22nd of March
  • Payments will be checked during 23th and 24th of March and players who have not paid the fee will be removed from the competition

Stage 3: 27th of March at 18.00 to 4th of April

  • All divisions and all ratings
  • Spots will be filled in registration order
  • Stage 3 registration payments must be received by the organizer by 7th of April
  • Payments will be checked during 11th - 12th of April and players who have not paid the fee will be removed from the competition

Stage 4: 13th of April

  • The amount of players in each divisions will be removed (if not full) and equally distributed into other divisions
  • Players will be added into the competition from the waiting list in the order of registration (division based)
  • Registration to competition will continue if there are still free spots

If a division reaches the maximum amount of players for example in phase 1 and a waiting list is formed, then the registration for that division is closed for later phases. That division will have its spots willed from the waiting list untill its empty. When(if) the waiting list is empty and there are free spots again in that division, then the registration will open again.
