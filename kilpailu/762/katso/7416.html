Hyvinkään Heinäpaali - Information in English

Hyvinkään Heinäpaali is a PDGA C-tier event. Event will be played on 15th and 16th of July (doubles on 14th). The very first Hyvinkään Heinäpaali was organized in 2014 and now it is time for 4th event. Heinäpaali will be played on two beautiful courses in Hyvinkää, Southern Finland. Sahanmäki DGP is said to be one the best forest courses in Finland and challenging Nummenmäki DGP will be the course for Finnish Nationals in 2017. Total of three rounds will be played in the event.

We'd like to welcome players outside Finland to Hyvinkään Heinäpaali!


GENERAL INFO
* 15.7. - 16.7.2017
* Hyvinkää, Finland
* PDGA C-tier
* PDGA membership required
* 3 x 21 holes
* Pool A: MA2, MA4, FA2, FA4 and MJ2
* Pool B: MPO, MPM, MPS and FPO
* Organized by Disc Golf Hyvinkää
* TD Mikko Wikman

SAHANMÄKI DGP
* Address Niinistönkatu, Hyvinkää
* Modified layout, 21 holes
* Round 1 will be played here

NUMMENMÄKI DGP
* Address Ilmailutie 5, Hyvinkää
* Normal layout with few changes (Three new holes to be build before event)
* Round 2 and 3 will be played here

REGISTRATION AND FEES
* Registration via SFL Kisakone (or contact TD)
* MPO, MPM, MPS and FPO 30,00 €
* MA2, MA4, FA2, FA4 and MJ2 15,00 €

PRIZES & TROPHIES
* MPO, MPM, MPS and FPO money prizes (TOP20%, payout 70%)
* MA2 and FA2 trophies and fun money TOP3
* MA4, FA4 and MJ2 trophies TOP3
* CTP competition for AMs on every round

ACCOMMODATION
