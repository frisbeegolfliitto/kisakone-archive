Event Info

  • PDGA A-tier
  • Divisions: MPO, MPM, MPG, FPO and FPM
  • 3 rounds of 18-21 holes for all
  • Tournament field of at least 144 players
  • Pick up doubles on Friday 15 May at 15-18 (GMT+2)

Schedule (subject to change)

  • Sat 08.00 round 1 pool B shotgun start
  • Sat 11.15 round 1 pool A shotgun start
  • Sat 14.30 round 2 pool B shotgun start
  • Sat 17.45 round 2 pool B shotgun start
  • Sun 08.00 round 3 start
  • Sun ~ 18.00 prize ceremony

Registration in Phases

  • A current PDGA membership or FDGA A-competition license required for all competitors
  • 50 € registration fee for MPO and 40 € in other divisions
  • Foreign registrations via email only to TD Marko Timonen email: marko.timonen@puuseppa.fi

Phase 1

  • 10 april 18.00 to 15 april 23.59 (GMT+2)
  • MPO minimum rating 960
  • MPM minimum rating 920
  • Others with any rating
  • In order of registration

Phase 2

  • 17 april 18.00 to 22 april 23.59 (GMT+2)
  • MPO minimum rating 940
  • Others with any rating
  • In order of registration

Phase 3

  • 24 april 18.00 to 30 april 23.59 (GMT+2)
  • Players added from possible waiting list
  • Registration open for all if available spaces
  • All division (except MPO) quotas will be lifted
