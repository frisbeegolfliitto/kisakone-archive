The Prodiscus After Easter Open is an annual spring classic at Viidentienristeys, Kankaanpää, Finland organized by the local club Tomoottajat Disc Golf. Since 2011 it has been the opener of the Finnish national PDGA tour calendar and established itself as one of the most popular events of the year. This time we welcome 90 of the best Finnish and European players to Kankaanpää for a two day pro only PDGA B tier event on 11-12 April 2015.

Event Info

  • PDGA B-tier
  • MPO and FPO
  • 3 rounds of 18 holes for all and a final round of 18 holes for the top 25 % in both divisions
  • tournament field of 90 players
  • pick up doubles on Friday 10 April at 15-18 (GMT+2)

Schedule (subject to change)

  • Sat 9.30 player's meeting
  • Sat 10.00 round 1 shotgun start
  • Sat 14.30 round 2 shotgun start
  • Sun 9.30 pick up cards
  • Sun 10.00 round 3 shotgun start
  • Sun 14.30 round 4 shotgun start for top 25 %
  • Sun ~ 18.00 prize ceremony

Registration in Phases

  • A current PDGA membership or FDGA competition license required for all competitors
  • 30 € registration fee for all divisions
  • Direct registration here

Phase 1

  • 1st March 18.00 (GMT+2) to 8 March (GMT+2)
  • MPO minimum rating 950
  • FPO any rating
  • in order of registration

Phase 2

  • 10 March 18.00 (GMT+2) to 16 March (GMT+2)
  • MPO any rating
  • FPO any rating
  • in order of registration

If all spots are filled, a waiting list is automatically established here.

Phase 3

  • 20 March 18.00 (GMT+2)
  • players added from possible waiting list
  • registration open for all if available spaces
  • wild cards (10) awarded

Sign up to register

 

  1. Click Rekisteröidy on the front page to begin registration.
  2. Enter your first name, last name and email address.
  3. Choose a user name and a password. Confirm password.
  4. Enter your PDGA number, sex (Mies=male, Nainen=woman), and year of birth
  5. Accept the standard terms and conditions
  6. Confirm by clickin Hyväksy
