In English

Welcome to the Prodiscus Open!

We are a PDGA B-tier this year, meaning we guarantee the event is well run and course groomed, and the prizes are right. Player pack will be available for all competitors.

We have reserved 8 spots in both MPO and MA2 and 2 spots in MPM and FPO for foreign players. This wildcard quota will expire on 10th July.

Joining the competition will cost 30 euros and we will have 70% payout over top 25% players in each division.

This year we have reserved a private hotel for the player's use. It will accomodate 40-50 players. Cost will be as low as possible. More details coming soon.

To participate, please email tournament director at kfghallitus@gmail.com with your name, division, pdga# and you will receive payment instructions.
