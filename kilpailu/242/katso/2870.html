The Grip EQ Tour, the Finnish National Disc Golf pro tour 2015

Lauste 4-5 July 2015

Event Info

  • PDGA international A-tier
  • pro only
  • 3 rounds of 18 holes for all
  • tournament field of 144 players
  • pick up doubles on Friday 3 July at 15-18 (GMT+2)

Schedule (subject to change)

  • Sat 7.30 player's meeting
  • Sat 8.00 Group A round 1 shotgun start
  • Sat 11.15 Group B player's meeting
  • Sat 11:30 Group B round 1 shotgun start
  • Sat 14.45 Group A round 2 shotgun start
  • Sat 18:00 Group B round 2 shotgun start
  • Sun 9:00 on golf starts every 10 mins
  • Sun ~ 18.00 prize ceremony

Registration in Phases

  • A current PDGA membership or FDGA competition license required for all competitors
  • 50 € registration fee in MPO, 40 € for all other divisions
  • Direct registration here

Phase 1

  • 27 May 12.00 (GMT+2) to 1 June 23:59 (GMT+2)
  • MPO minimum rating 960
  • MPM minimum rating 920
  • all other divisions all ratings
  • in order of registration

Phase 2

  • 3 June 12.00 (GMT+2) to 8 June 23:59 (GMT+2)
  • MPO minimum rating 940
  • all other divisions all ratings
  • in order of registration

If all spots are filled, a waiting list is automatically established here.

Phase 3

  • 10 June 12.00 (GMT+2) to 15 June 23:59 (GMT+2)
  • all other divisions all ratings
  • players added from possible waiting list

Sign up to register

 

  1. Click Rekisteröidy on the front page to begin registration.
  2. Enter your first name, last name and email address.
  3. Choose a user name and a password. Confirm password.
  4. Enter your PDGA number, sex (Mies=male, Nainen=woman), and year of birth
  5. Accept the standard terms and conditions
  6. Confirm by clickin Hyväksy
