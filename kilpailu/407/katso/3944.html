The Prodiscus After Easter Open is an annual spring classic at Viidentienristeys, Kankaanpää, Finland organized by the local club Tomoottajat Disc Golf. Since 2011 it has been the opener of the Finnish national PDGA tour calendar and established itself as one of the most popular events of the year. This time we welcome 252 of the best Finnish and European players to Kankaanpää for a two day pro only PDGA B tier event on 23-24 April 2016.

Event Info

  • PDGA B-tier
  • MPO and FPO
  • 2 rounds of 21+ holes for all
  • tournament field of 252 players
  • pick up doubles on Friday 22 April at 14-17 (GMT+2)

Schedule (subject to change)

  • Sat 8.00 player's meeting for pool A
  • Sat 8.30 round 1 shotgun start for pool A
  • Sat 12.00 player's meeting for pool B
  • Sat 12.30 round 1 shotgun start for pool B
  • Sat 16.00 player's meeting for pool C
  • Sat 16.30 round 1 shotgun start for pool C
  • Sun 8.00 player's meeting for pool A
  • Sun 8.30 round 2 shotgun start for pool A
  • Sun 12.00 player's meeting for pool B
  • Sun 12.30 round 2 shotgun start for pool B
  • Sun 16.00 player's meeting for pool C
  • Sun 16.30 round 2 shotgun start for pool C
  • Sun ~ 20.30 prize ceremony

Registration in Phases

  • A current PDGA membership or FDGA competition license required for all competitors
  • 30 € registration fee for MPO and 20 € for FPO
  • Direct registration here

Phase 1

  • 27 January 18.00 (GMT+2) to 31 January (GMT+2)
  • MPO minimum rating 930
  • FPO any rating
  • in order of registration

Phase 2

  • A quota of 20 foreing players reserved until the end of phase 2.
  • 7 February 18.00 (GMT+2) to 14 February (GMT+2)
  • MPO any rating
  • FPO any rating
  • in order of registration

If all spots are filled, a waiting list is automatically established.

Phase 3

  • 19 February 18.00 (GMT+2)
  • players added from possible waiting list
  • registration open for all if available spaces
  • wild cards (12) awarded

Sign up to register

 

  1. Click Rekisteröidy on the front page to begin registration.
  2. Enter your first name, last name and email address.
  3. Choose a user name and a password. Confirm password.
  4. Enter your PDGA number, sex (Mies=male, Nainen=woman), and year of birth
  5. Accept the standard terms and conditions
  6. Confirm by clickin Hyväksy
