KASTAPLAST THE BETTER WAY OF LIVING

Anytime is disc golf time… Rain, snow, wind or mud have got nothing on all you disc throwers. Your dedication and passion is what makes this game an unstoppable movement. It’s the better way of living!

At Kastaplast, we’re determined to supply you disc golf heroes with equipment that won’t let you down. Our mission is to create discs of highest quality, without shortcuts.

Kastaplast is an independent disc manufacturer based in Stockholm, Sweden. Click the About button to read the kasta-story. For latest updates, follow us on social media.
