Wherever work takes you

Welcome to new normal.

Working life is changing faster than ever. What was once a workplace is now a coffeehouse by the sea or an apartment in your favourite city. What was once a foreign land is now a potential work site full of opportunities. Today you can take your work wherever you want to go or take on new work wherever you are.

Wherever the Project Takes You

Stay affordably

When a project takes the team far from home, accommodation becomes an important factor in terms of physical and psychic well-being as well as coping at work. Forenom arranges comfortable, affordable and homely accommodation close to the construction site, where your employees can recover from the working day and rest properly before the next one.

Wherever inspiration takes You

Try a new city

Do you feel like a little change of scenery? Or do you dream about a whole new life in a different city? Now it’s easier than ever to try it. Pack your laptop and follow your inspiration to wherever it takes you. Tip your toes in new life, marvel and get inspired, find your place in the world. Forenom’s home-like apartments, aparthotels and rooms let you experience your new surroundings from the local’s point of view.

Wherever Life Takes You

Stay comfortable and safe

At Forenom we have done everything to make sure you can hit the road with peace of mind. In addition to our normal additional services, such as breakfast, cleaning and parking, we have developed new services for the post-corona situation and to facilitate teleworking. For example, you can order a desk and an office chair in your apartment. Wireless internet is often included in the price.
