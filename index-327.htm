THE HOTEL WITH ITS OWN GHOST

The longest-serving “employee” of Hotel Seurahuone is the maid Elsi, who started her career early in the 20th century. The beautiful maid Elsi fell in love with the hotel owner, but the love story ended tragically because of their social class differences. Disappointed in love, Elsi still wanders the hotel corridors, rides the elevator, and plays small tricks. The ghost is harmless but mischievous. If the lights flicker or the kitchen machines start on their own, it is very often Elsi who is behind it. Let us know if you see her whiles your stay with us!
